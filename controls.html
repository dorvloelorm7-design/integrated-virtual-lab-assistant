VR — Meta Quest

Left thumbstick Move and strafe around the lab
Right controller Emits a cyan laser pointer — aim it at objects to interact
Right trigger
(chat closed)		 Open the AI chat panel for the nearest station, or aim the laser at a glowing orb first to pick a specific station
Right trigger
(chat open)		 Start voice input · press again to stop and send
Left trigger Close the chat panel · or stop the AI from speaking
Aim at glowing orb Laser highlights the orb; press right trigger to open that station's chat
Aim at green screen Point at the LabMate tablet screen; press right trigger to see the website URL in your HUD
How interaction works in VR:
Walk near any glowing coloured orb (each one marks a lab station). A message appears in the floating HUD panel in front of you. Press the right trigger to open the chat panel — it appears as a 2D overlay inside the headset. You can then type with the Quest virtual keyboard, or press the right trigger again to speak your question. When the AI answers you will hear it read aloud and see the text in both the HUD and the chat panel. Press the left trigger to close the chat and walk to the next station.
